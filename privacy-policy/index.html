Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026
Application: A&Co Vera (Internal QuickBooks Integration)
Operator: Ardent & Co., Miami, FL

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ardent & Co. ("we," "us," or "our") handles data accessed through the A&Co Vera application, an internal tool that connects to Intuit QuickBooks Online via the Intuit Developer API.

2. Data Accessed

A&Co Vera accesses QuickBooks Online data solely for internal accounting and financial management purposes on behalf of Ardent & Co. and its affiliated entities. This data may include:

  • Chart of accounts and account balances
  • Transaction records (income, expenses, invoices, bills)
  • Vendor and customer records
  • Financial reports

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed through A&Co Vera is used exclusively for internal business operations, including accounting review, reconciliation, and financial reporting. We do not use this data for marketing, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose beyond internal operations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose QuickBooks data to any third party, except as required by law or to authorized personnel within Ardent & Co. for legitimate business purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

OAuth credentials (access tokens and refresh tokens) are stored securely on private internal servers with restricted access. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect all data. No financial data is stored externally beyond what Intuit's own platform retains.

6. Data Retention

We retain OAuth credentials only as long as the application is in active use. Upon decommissioning of the application, all credentials and cached data are securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

As this application is for internal use only, end users are employees and contractors of Ardent & Co. Any questions about data access or deletion may be directed to megan@ardentandco.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
